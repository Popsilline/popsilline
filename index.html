About Me

Building worlds
from the surface in.

"The texture of a surface tells the story of what something has been through."

I'm a 3D artist specialising in texturing, look development, and modeling — Completed my Bachelor of Design (Animation) at Whistling Woods International, Mumbai.

As Lead Texturing Artist on multiple fully realised student productions, I've worked across the entire CG pipeline: from UV layout and shader graphs to scene lighting, storyboarding, and compositing. My short film Anomaly won Best Animation at the Mumbai Short International Film Festival 2025 and reached audiences at IIT Bombay's First Film Fest and Chitkara IAFF 2026.

I recently interned at Studio EEKSAURUS as a Lighting & Texturing Artist on Project Aurobindo, where I painted hand-crafted HDRIs, lit shots under production timelines, and adapted to evolving visual styles mid-pipeline.
